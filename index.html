// shensuo.ai · founder & builder · in production

  • Multi-engine prompt pipeline across GPT-class, Gemini, and Perplexity
  • Severity scoring that flags high-mention / negative-narrative gaps
  • Full SaaS: auth, dashboards, alerting, Stripe billing, live deploy
